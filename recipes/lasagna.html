Lasagna

Description

Beautiful homemade lasagna recipe that only takes minutes to put together and cook. Granted, those minutes add up to hours when you consider making and simmering the sauce (for hours) then the wonderful act of boiling water and cooking noodles. Please don't forget the time it will take for you to take those hot noodles out of the colander... well we will get to that later

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Pesto
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Minced or Chopped Garlic

Steps

Sauce

  1. Add tomato sauce to pot, put on a low simmer
  2. Add seasonings and Garlic to sauce
  3. Brown ground beef
  4. Add Ground beef to sauce
  5. Simmer sauce for as many hours as you have (3hrs minimum)

Pasta

Prepare the noodles as directed from the box directions

Construct the Lasagna

  1. Using a ladle, place a small amount of sauce to cover bottom of the baking dish
  2. Layer 4 pieces of lasagna noodles over first sauce layer
  3. Add more sauce on top of noodles
  4. Add a mixture of Mozzarella, Italian, and Parmesan chesses over sauce
  5. Spoon small dabs of Ricotta Cheese onto layer
  6. Repeat steps 2 through 5 until you have a top layer that is Cheese
  7. Cover dish and place in 350 degree oven for 30 minutes
  8. Uncover and bake for 15 more minutes
