Golden Mushroom Beef

Description

Get rid of your crock pot, throw your pressure cooker and air fryer in the trash and go get a cast iron dutch oven. Its about time you learned how to cook lazy using real kitchen tools and simple ass ingredients

Ingredients

  • 3lbs of beef (you don't need to spend a lot here)
  • 2 cans Golden Mushroom Soup
  • 1 Envelope Onion Soup Mix
  • 1 Bottle of red wine (go ahead and pour a glass)

Steps

  1. Salt/pepper/season the beef
  2. Brown the beef in the dutch oven on the stove top
  3. Remove from heat
  4. Add Soup and Soup Mix
  5. Add wine so the meat is almost covered, but don't drown it
  6. Cover the dutch oven
  7. Place in 300 degree oven for 2 1/2 - 3 hours
  8. Stick a fork in it, if its falling apart tender its done

Serve over Rice, or Potatoes, or Egg Noodles, or Biscuits
