Chili

Description

This is the chili recipe that my grandfather used at his hamburger stand, butchered and changed over the years as my mom learned it from her mother in law. No beans baby, this one is for slopping on burgers and dogs and scooping with chips.

Ingredients

  • 3 -3.5lbs Ground Beef
  • 1/2 tube of sausage (Jimmy Dean)
  • 29oz of tomato sauce (but have some extra on hand)
  • Chili Powder (get the big one, you will use most of it)
  • Black Pepper
  • Red Pepper
  • Crushed Red Pepper

Steps

  1. Add Ground Beef to a cold pot
  2. Add Sausage
  3. Add tomato Sauce
  4. Set burner to low
  5. Add Seasonings
  6. Mix everything together
  7. Slow cook (uncovered) for several hours
  8. Stir often
  9. After an hour taste, and add more seasonings (the more chili powder the better)
